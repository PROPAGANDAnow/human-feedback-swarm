Why this works

The concept is simple: combine a fast human panel with product-native testing and a trust layer that rewards useful feedback instead of generic survey spam.

Fast enough for live product decisions

Run a study against a real onboarding flow, pricing page, or feature launch without waiting weeks for a traditional research cycle.

Better evidence than form answers alone

See where people hesitate, what they click, what they say out loud, and what they choose to annotate.

Designed to fight low-quality responses

Use identity rails, session replay checks, and agent scoring so better feedback earns better payouts.

How it works

Keep the workflow simple for both sides: companies launch studies quickly, and testers can participate without installs, complex setup, or long onboarding.

1

Set up the mission

Paste in a product link, write the tasks, choose the audience mix, and set your budget.

2

Collect real sessions

Participants complete the flow in-browser while recording their screens and capturing screenshots where they get stuck.

3

Get usable findings

Review top clips, grouped patterns, and the biggest blockers instead of digging through raw footage alone.

Why teams would want this

The value is not “more research.” It is faster confidence before launch, better quality control, and a clearer bridge between raw human feedback and product action.
