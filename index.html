Four pillars of the platform

The concept centers on real humans, real product environments, and strong anti-gaming mechanisms — not synthetic tests or low-quality survey farms.

1. Product-native testing

Upload a staging URL or prototype and wrap it in a guided sidebar for tasks, prompts, and screenshots.

  • In-browser screen capture
  • Task flows + timed checkpoints
  • Inline screenshot annotation

2. Human quality + trust

Verify participants with identity, behavior, and agent review so reward dollars go to authentic sessions.

  • World ID / sybil resistance
  • Quality scoring from agents
  • Optional camera / attention tier

3. Flexible incentive engine

Pay more for stronger evidence, niche expertise, or richer modalities like camera-on walkthroughs.

  • $5–$50 quality-based rewards
  • Credit rewards for trusted ecosystems
  • Reduced incentive farming

Why this is differentiated

This is not just another survey tool — it combines product-native tasks, verified humans, dynamic incentives, and decision-ready synthesis.

Trust, incentives, supply

Design the economics so quality wins

The notes emphasized avoiding low-effort farming. The prototype therefore models a quality-weighted payout system with optional higher-value modalities.

Identity + sybil resistance

World ID or equivalent identity rails reduce duplicate accounts, fake cohorts, and resold sessions.

Agent-based authenticity checks

Replay the browser session, parse the narration, and compare evidence quality before finalizing payment.

Tiered incentives

Reward richer evidence with higher payout: screen-only, screen + voice, camera-on, or specialist review.
